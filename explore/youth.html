Youth

Learn about Newtonian physics on our bumper swing, or travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth! Our exhibits are accessible for all interested minds, so come ready to learn and explore.

If you would like to learn and explore with a team of experts, join our Young Stars Club. The Young Stars Club meets every Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00

Our Holiday clubs meet during School Holidays and they provide the perfect opportunity to explore with other kids in your age group. Each holiday we pick a new theme to explore.

Contact us for more information or to sign-up for a club.
